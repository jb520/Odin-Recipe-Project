Fruit and Cream Sandwich

Description

It's a really good sandwich/dessert. Plus I could never forget how I somehow managed to full on spill this all over my husband's pants the first time I made it for us on a picnic.

Ingredients

  • Fruit of choice
  • Whipping cream
  • Sugar or condensed milk
  • Fluffy bread (like milk bread)

Steps

  1. Wash and cut fruit into desired size
  2. Set aside
  3. In a bowl wisk cream (by hand or with a hand mixer, stand mixer, etc) until stiff peaks form
  4. Once cream is almost set drizzle in condensed milk (just eyeball it)
  5. Fold condensed milk into whipped cream, if it falls/breaks just whip it into shape again
  6. Place cream into fridge to set (I want to say like 15-30 minutes maybe but i don't really know)
  7. Aquire your bread and place a generous spread of cream on one slice
  8. Layer fruit over cream, try not to overstuff the sandwich
  9. Layer more cream and top with second bread slice
  10. Wrap sandwich in plastic wrap and maybe let chill for a little bit for besat results
  11. For cuteness, cut sandwich (still in plastic wrap for structure) on a diagonal and hopefully it'll look really cute and yummy
  12. Enjoy! (:
