Checkerboard Cookies

Description

Square cookies made of chocolate and vanilla dough in an alternating pattern! So cute!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Cream together sugar and softened butter
  2. Add eggs
  3. Add vanilla extract
  4. Add baking powder, salt, and flour
  5. Mix or knead until a shaggy dough forms
  6. Separate dough into two equal halves
  7. Roll one half into a log and shape into long rectangle
  8. Wrap the rectangle in plastic wrap and place in the fridge to chill (This will be your vanilla dough!)
  9. With remaining half of dough, add cocoa powder
  10. Mix or knead until color is even throughout
  11. Repeat log to rectangle shaping process and place in fridge to chill (You guessed it! The chocolate dough!)
  12. After both doughs have chilled for ~2hrs, remove them from the fridge and cut into four strips each
    • The easiest way for me to do it is cut in half the long way and then cut those halves in half again
    • You're aiming for long square shaped strips
  13. Reassemble the cookie logs by stacking the doughs in an alternanting pattern
  14. Press franken logs together for better adhesion, re-wrap in plastic wrap and chill in the fridge for ~1hr
  15. When ready to bake simply remove them from the fridge, slice into cookie sized pieces and bake!
